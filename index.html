ENERGY DAY

Energy Day is the flagship annual event of the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay, celebrated over two exciting days. It serves as a platform to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange in the field of energy. The event brings together students, researchers, industry professionals, and academicians to explore emerging trends, cutting-edge technologies, and sustainable solutions for energy challenges.Energy Day features an engaging lineup of keynote lectures, panel discussions, technical exhibitions, workshops, and competitions, creating a dynamic environment for learning and networking. It is a celebration of ideas and a step towards shaping a sustainable energy future.
