Renewable Energy and Sustainability

Energy Day is The Flagship Annual Event Of The Department Of Energy Science And Engineering (DESE) Bombay, celebrated every Year during Spring Semester. Energy Day serves as a platform to foster knowledge Exchange in Energy field and its interdisciplinary Applications. Energy Day brings together Experts From Academia, Industry, Research Institute, Energy Policy Partnerships And Government to discuss And Examine solutions for Energy Challenges Around the Program on Engaging, Thought-provoking Discussions on cutting-edge Research And technological And Environmental, Emerging Trends And International Cooperation In Energy Policy. The Event Also Showcases Students Research, Learning And Networking. It's A Celebration Of Ideas And A Step Towards Building A Sustainable Energy Future.
