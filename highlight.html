Energy Day 2024
Highlights

Guest Lecture

As part of Energy Day, we were privileged to host Dr. Mahesh Patankar for a guest lecture. His lecture on modelling the energy transition was a highlight of Energy Day. The lecture stimulated engaging discussions and provided a framework for understanding the complexities of the energy transition.

Hackathon

Energy Day at IIT Bombay hosted the Energenius Hackathon in 2024, focusing on "Hacking Energy Challenges with AI & ML." The hackathon saw enthusiastic participation from students eager to apply their skills in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to solve real-world energy problems.

Panel Discussion

Energy Day hosted a dynamic panel discussion exploring the ambitious goal of India achieving net-zero emissions by 2070. The discussion brought together leading experts to debate the challenges, opportunities, and strategies for reaching this target.
Panelist:

  • Shailesh Deshpande, Vice President, Burns & McDonnell India
  • Anvesha Thakker, Partner & Lead Renewable Energy, KPMG India
  • Naresh Bazzar, Co-founder, GSE Renewables
  • Ajay Phatak, Strategic Technical, Dastur Energy
  • Trupti Mishra, Prof. SJSOM, IIT Bombay

    • Case Competition

    The Case Study Competition brought together innovative minds to tackle real-world energy challenges. Participants engaged in data-driven problem-solving to explore groundbreaking solutions for sustainability, electric mobility, and energy security.
    Problem Statement:
    1. Leveraging the Mpemba Effect Exploring the Mpemba Effect to develop innovative methods for accelerating cooling and heating processes in industries, focusing on efficiency and sustainability.
    2. Sustainable Shift: A Holistic Examination of the 100% EV Transition in India A holistic assessment of India's transition to electric vehicles, addressing sustainability, infrastructure challenges, and policy recommendations.
    3. Cyber-safe Power Grids Investigating the vulnerabilities of power grids to cyber threats and developing resilient, cost-effective cybersecurity strategies.
